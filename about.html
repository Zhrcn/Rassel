About RASEEL

Building excellence in Saudi Arabia since 2009 AD

Our Story

Established: 2009 AD

Founder: Eng. Firas Fathi Ahmad Al-Dawalibi

RASEEL Innovation Company for General Contracting was established by Engineer Firas Fathi Ahmad Al-Dawalibi after several years of experience in the field of construction. Today, we are taking confident steps to provide our clients with integrated solutions for all construction projects.

Our driving force is passion, commitment and contribution to economic growth, and to be part of the 2030 vision of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud and Crown Prince Muhammad bin Salman Al Saud - may God protect them.
