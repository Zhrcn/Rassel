About Us

Established: 2009 AD

Founder: Eng. Firas Fathi Ahmad Al-Dawalibi

RASEEL Innovation Company for General Contracting was established by Engineer Firas Fathi Ahmad Al-Dawalibi after several years of experience in the field of construction. Today, we are taking confident steps to provide our clients with integrated solutions for all construction projects.

Our driving force is passion, commitment and contribution to economic growth, and to be part of the 2030 vision of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud and Crown Prince Muhammad bin Salman Al Saud - may God protect them.

Our Vision

Today, we are taking confident steps to provide our clients with integrated solutions for all construction projects. Our driving force is passion, commitment, and contribution to economic growth, and to be part of the 2030 vision of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud and Crown Prince Muhammad bin Salman Al Saud — may God protect them. We keep pace with the enormous expansion and development in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, moving at a steady pace to build a homeland that is a successful and pioneering model at all levels. Our success lies in the diversity of our field and our simple philosophy of absolute transparency, which builds confidence between us, suppliers, and workers, stemming from a constant passion for continuous development and contributing to educating the community about the origins of authentic architecture.

Our Mission

To be an essential element in our clients' lives by providing superior design and services to help you achieve your aspirations. Everything we do revolves around you. We strive to keep you at your best and remain loyal to you and your time. RASEEL Innovation is the link between dreams and reality — to become the most trusted company and bring inspiration and innovation to every corner of the world.
